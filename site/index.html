How it works

1) Scan or search

Scan any product or enter a product, brand, or company name — no barcode or photo required.

2) Explore the story

Learn about ownership, where products are made, how materials are sourced, and how many Canadians a business employs.

3) Find alternatives

Discover independently curated Canadian alternatives with direct links to brand websites.
